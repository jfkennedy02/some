Frequently asked questions

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with a wide range of businesses—from small startups to large enterprises—across various industries including healthcare, finance, retail, and education.

Do you offer 24/7 support?

Yes. Our team provides round-the-clock monitoring and emergency support to ensure your systems stay up and running at all times.

How long does a typical network installation take?

Project timelines vary depending on the size and complexity of the setup, but most small to mid-size network installations are completed within 2–5 business days.

Can you help secure my business from cyber threats?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive cybersecurity services, including firewall installation, malware protection, threat monitoring, and employee training.

Do you provide remote IT support?

Yes. Our technicians can remotely access and troubleshoot your systems to resolve issues quickly without needing to be on-site.

Is there a contract required for your services?

Not always. We offer both one-time service options and ongoing maintenance contracts depending on what best suits your business.

How do I request a quote or consultation?

You can fill out the form on our Contact Us page, call us directly, or click the "Request a Quote" button for a free consultation.

Can you upgrade or repair existing IT infrastructure?

Yes. Whether it's replacing outdated hardware, improving network speed, or securing your current systems, we can assess and enhance your existing setup.
